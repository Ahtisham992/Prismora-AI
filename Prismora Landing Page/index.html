Process

From URL to Reel
in 6 Simple Steps

Paste any podcast URL and let Prismora AI handle everything — transcription, summarization, and highlight generation.

1
Upload URL
Paste any YouTube or podcast link. We fetch the media automatically.
2
Transcription
OpenAI Whisper converts speech to accurate, timestamped text.
3
AI Summary
PEGASUS/BART NLP models extract key insights and summaries.
4
Keyframe Detection
OpenCV detects scene changes and visual events in the video.
5
Highlight Generation
NLP + CV fusion creates short, meaningful reel clips automatically.
6
Review & Share
Edit, post, and share your highlights directly from the mobile app.
Features

Everything You Need
to Master Podcasts

A complete platform combining AI transcription, smart search, community curation, and personalized feeds.

AI Transcription with Timestamps
Powered by OpenAI Whisper, every word is transcribed with precise timestamps. Search any topic across all your podcasts instantly.
Instant Highlight Reels
Auto-generated short clips of the most impactful moments, exported as MP4/MOV.
Global Smart Search
Full-text Elasticsearch search across all transcripts. Find any topic in any podcast instantly.
Personalized Feed
Collaborative filtering recommends content based on your listening history and preferences.
Community Curation
Upvote, annotate, and validate highlights collaboratively. Community-verified accuracy.
